Instruction:

For classic egg salad, hard-boil and chop six eggs. Mix 1/4 cup mayo, 1 tsp Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Optional: add chopped celery or green onions. Combine with eggs, adjust seasoning, and refrigerate if desired. Serve on bread or as a topping. Customize with herbs or pickles. Enjoy!
